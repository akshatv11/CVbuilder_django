BUILD YOUR RESUME HERE...

The designed app will help the college students to make and update their C.V. throughout their college studies and college authorities will be able to record their progress and C.V. After creating the C.V the students will be able to download their C.V. for future reference. The app can also be used as a recruitment platform for teachers, where interested teachers who are looking for teaching role can upload their C.V on this app and the college authorities will be able to shortlist candidates for teaching position according to their C.V.
